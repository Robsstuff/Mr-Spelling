The Rule

PLURAL RULE 1
word + S = plural

The simplest and most common plural rule in English. To make most nouns plural, just add -S to the end of the word. This rule works for the vast majority of English nouns.

cat → cats hat → hats dog → dogs frog → frogs car → cars star → stars
